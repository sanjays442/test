Luxury is not at the forefront of most of these programs.  They're about helping troubled youths with tough love and real treatment.  They also often allow clients to continue their studies so as to not fall behind in school after they graduate from the program.

Wilderness camps provide a lot of benefits for both adolescents seeking treatment, and their parents.  Some of those benefits include:

  • Structure.  Wilderness camps are set up with very strict structure.  Often a structure that is lacking at home.  This allows younger clients to learn a way of life without drugs and alcohol.  It also provides parents with guidelines for implimenting structure when their adolescent returns home.
  • Adventure. While wilderness camps can be challenging to teens, they also provide a great sense of adventure during the recovery process.
  • Beautiful setting. Most wilderness camps are located in the forest or in the mountains giving clients a great natural environment to begin their journey to recovery.

Wilderness camps are a great option for troubled teens and often young adults alike.
