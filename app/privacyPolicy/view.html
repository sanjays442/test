Addiction Network Privacy Policy


Addiction Network (also known in this document as “we,” “us,” and “our”) is a listing of treatment providers for drug and alcohol abuse. Addiction Network takes steps to ensure the privacy of our clients and website visitors. This page explains our Online Privacy Policy and the information we gather from visitors. To view our Online Privacy Policy, click the visible link at the bottom of each page of the site. To protect unauthorized viewers from accessing the personal information of visitors, Addiction Network uses SSL Encryption.

Collecting User Information Addiction Network collects the names, email addresses, and phone numbers of users who contact its site through email. We also collect other personal information at the client’s discretion.

Using Personal Information Addiction Network collects personal information from email forms to provide requested information and answer emails. It does not share or sell personal information collected on such forms to third parties. Addiction Network uses such information only for its own use and it is only available to its employees. Addiction Network does not permit the use of personal information in any other way.

Protecting the Privacy of Children Addiction Network is a listing of treatment providers. Many treatment providers and their sites are designed for visitors 18 years old and older. We did not design our site to attract visitors under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from people under the age of 18.
